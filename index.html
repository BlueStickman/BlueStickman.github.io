Modded Among us

Introduction


Among Us- Among us is a game of trust and betrayal.There are 2 roles in the game:


1:Crewmate


To win in this role you have to either finish your tasks or vote/eject the impostor out

In this role you have to be carefull for the crewmates might eject you if you act sus enough


2:Impostor


To win in this role you have to either kill all the crewmates or win by sabotage

In this role you have to be carefull for you might get killed by the impostor(s) or get voted out if you act sus enough


Among Us Mods

An among us mod is a modification to the game.For example , instead of having only the 2 normal roles , you make a new one .

For you to play an Among us mod , you need a server that won't ban you thinking you are cheating

So for you to play modded among us , you need a new server.Not only a new server but a modded client

If you wanna get some mods , that have been made , go to the Mods page and learn how to set it up
